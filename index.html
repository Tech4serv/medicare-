Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a prescription?

Yes, all prescription medications require a valid prescription from a licensed physician. We can help you transfer your existing prescription or connect you with a telemedicine service for a consultation.

How long does shipping take?

Priority Mail takes 3-5 business days ($45), and Overnight shipping takes 1-2 business days ($189). Both include same-day tracking and discreet packaging.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! We use PayPal's secure payment processing with advanced encryption. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

What if I have issues with my order?

Contact our 24/7 support team via phone, email, or WhatsApp. We'll resolve any issues within 24 hours. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.

How do I know the medications are authentic?

All our medications are sourced directly from FDA-approved manufacturers and licensed wholesalers. We provide certificates of authenticity upon request.

Can I return my medication?

Due to safety regulations, we cannot accept returns on prescription medications. However, if there's an issue with your order, please contact us immediately for resolution.
